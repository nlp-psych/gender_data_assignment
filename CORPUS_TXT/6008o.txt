Routines---They are the organizationally specific 'ways we do things around here' which tend to persist over time and guide people's behaviour. Political processes---------There may be a need for the reconfiguration of power structures in the organisation to build a power base, encourage support or overcome resistance and achieve commitment to a strategy or course of action.
